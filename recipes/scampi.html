Shrimp Scampi

Homemade Shrimp Scampi Recipe

This recipe was introduced in Italy, before making it's impact nationwide!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine or broth
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 3/4 pounds large or extra-large shrimp, shelled
  • 1/3 cup chopped parsley
  • Half of lemon for juice
  • Cooked pasta

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter with olive oil. Add garlic and saute until fragrant. (1 minute)
  2. Add wine or broth, salt, red pepper flakes, and plenty of black pepper, bring to a simmer. Letting wine reduce by half. (approx. 2 minutes)
  3. Add shrimp and saute until they turn pink. (2-4 minutes) Stir in the parsley and lemon juice, you are ready to serve!
