Homemade Lobster Recipe

Best sea foods!

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves(chopped or grated)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
  • Pinch of Cayenne pepper
  • Four 8oz Lobster tails
  • Kosher salt

Steps:

  1. Brush the grill with oil. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium-low heat. Add the garlic, bringing to a sizzle for 30 seconds. Remove from heat and stir in zest, and juice. After it cooled for 5 minutes stir in parsley, chives, and cayenne.
  2. Cut the lobster shells, pulling them apart slightly to expose meat. Brush all over with oil, and lightly salt.
  3. Grill the tails meat side down, for about 4-5 minutes. Flipping and rebuttering. Cover the lid and grill untill the lobster meat turns white(3 minutes)
  4. Serve!
