Hot Wings

Homemade Style Wings

One of the best foods ever created in my opinion! Originated in NYC

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 pounds chicken wings
  • 3/4 cup Frank's RedHot Buffalo Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons bourbon
  • 2 tablespoons pancake syrup

Steps:

  1. Bake wings in foil-lined pan at 450 degrees, on lowest oven rack. (30 minutes)
  2. Mix Frank's, bourbon, and syrup in saucepan, heat until warm.
  3. Toss wings in sauce to serve!
